George Brown College:

  The college offers 35 diploma programs, 
31 advanced diploma programs as well as eight degree programs,
one in conjunction with Ryerson University

  As of September 2020 there are ~26000 full-time students, 
inculding ~3500 international and ~3800 part-time students 
   
GBC was chartered in 1996 by the government of Ontario and opened the next year.
